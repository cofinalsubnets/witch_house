witch_house

witch_house is a MUD-like multiplayer world-building environment written in Haskell and (somewhat) scriptable in a purpose-made miniature Lisp.

how to use

Build. Run. Connect using telnet or netcat. Invite your friends, teleport into their bodies, and swap their organs. Program them to weep with joy in response. Repeat.

how to write wisp

Wisp is the little Lisp embedded in witch_house. Its vocabulary is vaguely Scheme-like. More documentation may be forthcoming; meantime, running witch_house --wisp will start up a REPL.
